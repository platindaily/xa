FAQ

Everything you need to know about the National Football League — its teams, records, and legacy.

When was the NFL founded?

The NFL was founded in 1920 as the American Professional Football Association (APFA) and renamed the National Football League in 1922. It has grown from 10 teams to 32 franchises across the United States.

Which team has the most Super Bowl victories?

The Pittsburgh Steelers and the New England Patriots are tied for the most Super Bowl wins, each holding six championships as of now.

Who holds the record for the most career passing yards?

Drew Brees and Tom Brady have both set monumental passing yard records, with Brady ultimately surpassing 85,000 yards during his career.

What is the oldest NFL franchise still operating?

The Arizona Cardinals, founded in 1898 (originally the Morgan Athletic Club), are considered the oldest continuously run professional football team in America.

How many teams are in the NFL?

The NFL currently consists of 32 teams divided equally between the American Football Conference (AFC) and the National Football Conference (NFC).

When did the Super Bowl era begin?

The Super Bowl era started with Super Bowl I on January 15, 1967, when the Green Bay Packers defeated the Kansas City Chiefs.

Who has the most career rushing yards?

Emmitt Smith holds the all-time record with 18,355 rushing yards during his career with the Dallas Cowboys and Arizona Cardinals.

What is the purpose of the NFL Draft?

The NFL Draft allows teams to select eligible college players to join the league, helping maintain competitive balance and develop new talent.

Where is the NFL Hall of Fame located?

The Pro Football Hall of Fame is located in Canton, Ohio — the same city where the NFL was founded in 1920.

How can I watch live NFL games?

You can watch NFL games through official broadcasters such as CBS, FOX, NBC, ESPN, and NFL Network, or stream them on authorized platforms like NFL+, YouTube TV, and Prime Video.

What makes the NFL season unique?

The NFL regular season spans 18 weeks, followed by the playoffs and culminating in the Super Bowl — one of the world’s most-watched sporting events.

Can I view past championship stats on this site?

Yes, our “Leaderboard” and “Blog” sections highlight key moments, historic records, and stats from past NFL seasons for quick reference and learning.
