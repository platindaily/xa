Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

1. Introduction

At NFL History (“we”, “us”, or “our”), your privacy is important to us. Our goal is to create a safe, informative, and enjoyable space for fans to explore the rich legacy of professional football. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information while you browse, read, or interact with content across the site.

2. Information We Collect

  • Browsing data: We do not collect personal identifiers. Basic non-personal information (like browser type or screen size) may be used to optimize your experience and site performance.
  • Local preferences: Some settings, such as your preferred theme or saved viewing mode, may be stored in your browser’s localStorage. This data stays on your device.
  • Contact form: If you choose to reach out via our contact section, we will receive the details you provide (such as your name, email, and message) for the sole purpose of responding to your inquiry.

3. How We Use Information

  • To ensure smooth navigation and display of NFL History pages and archives.
  • To maintain and improve website features and accessibility for all football fans.
  • To reply to any questions or comments you send through our contact form.

4. Cookies and Analytics

NFL History does not use tracking cookies or analytics by default. If we introduce analytics or partnership integrations in the future (for example, to measure content performance or fan engagement), we will update this policy and allow you to review those options.

5. Children’s Privacy

NFL History is suitable for general audiences, but football fans under 13 should browse with parental guidance. We encourage parents and guardians to discuss online safety with children while exploring sports content together.

6. Data Retention

Local data (like theme or score preferences) remains in your browser until you clear it manually. Contact messages are retained only as long as needed to respond and maintain brief correspondence records.

7. Your Choices

  • Manage or delete saved preferences using your browser’s site data settings.
  • Browse in private or incognito mode for sessions that don’t save local data.
  • Contact us if you wish to have a previously sent message deleted from our records.

8. Third-Party Links

NFL History may include references to official NFL sources, team pages, or media archives. We do not control these external sites and are not responsible for their privacy practices or content once you leave our platform.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to keep browsing safe. However, please remember that no online platform can guarantee complete security across the internet.

10. International Visitors

NFL History may be accessed by fans from around the world. By visiting the site, you consent to information being processed in the country where our servers operate, following this Privacy Policy.

11. Updates to This Policy

From time to time, we may make updates to this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements, new features, or compliance requirements. The “Last updated” date at the top shows the most recent revision.

12. Contact

Have questions about privacy or data use? Reach out to us through the contact section of our website, and our team will respond promptly.
